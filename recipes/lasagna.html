Lasagna

Description

A delicious recipe for many different occassions. This lasagna will melt in your mouth and leave all your guests speechless.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Noodles
  • Tomato sauce of your choice
  • Cheese
  1. Pre-heat oven
  2. Cook ground beef
  3. line noodles in bottom of pan
  4. add meat on noodles
  5. add sauce on meat
  6. repeat 3 steps above until out of ingredients
  7. bake
  8. top with cheese and bake a little longer
